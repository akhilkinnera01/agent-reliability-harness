# Laboratory Safety Manual

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the safety procedures for our chemistry laboratory.
All personnel must follow these guidelines at all times.

## 2. Chemical Handling

### 2.1 General Requirements
When working with chemicals, ensure proper ventilation is maintained.
Handle all substances carefully and avoid direct contact.
Store chemicals in designated areas according to compatibility.

### 2.2 Protective Equipment
Wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) at all times.
This includes lab coats, safety goggles, and gloves.

## 3. Equipment Operation

### 3.1 Training
Before using any laboratory equipment, complete the required training program.
Certification is required for operating specialized instruments.

### 3.2 Operation
Follow manufacturer guidelines for all equipment operations.
Report any malfunctions to your supervisor immediately.

## 4. Emergency Procedures

### 4.1 Fire
In case of fire, activate the nearest fire alarm.
Evacuate the building using designated exit routes.
Do not use elevators during evacuation.

### 4.2 Chemical Spills
In case of chemical spills, follow cleanup procedures immediately.
Contact the safety officer for assistance with hazardous materials.
Evacuate the area if toxic fumes are present.

## 5. Waste Disposal

All chemical waste must be disposed of according to regulations.
Use designated containers for different types of waste.
Never pour chemicals down the drain without authorization.
